## 📧 Email Setup Recommendations

Once you have your domain:

### Professional Email Options

1. **Google Workspace** ($6/user/month)
   - Gmail interface
   - Google Drive, Docs, Calendar
   - Professional addresses: `hello@microplanner.ai`

2. **Zoho Mail** ($1/user/month or FREE for 5 users)
   - Affordable
   - Good email client
   - Great for startups

3. **Cloudflare Email Routing** (FREE)
   - Forward `*@yourdomain.com` to personal email
   - No sending capability
   - Good for early stage

## 🛡️ Domain Security

Once you register:

- ✅ Enable **domain privacy/WHOIS protection**
- ✅ Enable **2FA** on registrar account
- ✅ Enable **domain locking** (prevent unauthorized transfers)
- ✅ Use **Cloudflare** for DNS (free DDoS protection, CDN, analytics)
- ✅ Set up **DNSSEC** for extra security
